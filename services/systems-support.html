Home & Office IT Support

Technology that just works — set up, maintained, and supported by people who explain things in plain English. For homes and businesses across Long Island.

Your Technology, Taken Care Of

Whether you're working from home, running a small business, or just want your family's devices to work without headaches — we're here to help. MyCo IT provides hands-on, personal technology support that feels like having a tech-savvy friend on speed dial.

What We Help With

From new computer setup to ongoing maintenance, we handle it all:

  • Computer and device setup, configuration, and optimization
  • Operating system updates, troubleshooting, and repairs
  • Email setup and productivity tool configuration
  • Data backup solutions and recovery planning
  • Printer, scanner, and peripheral setup
  • Smart home device integration
  • Software installation and licensing guidance

Security That Protects You

We protect your devices and data with the same tools big companies use — but explained in plain English. From antivirus and malware protection to secure password management and two-factor authentication, we make sure you're covered without making it complicated.

Remote & On-Site Support

Many issues can be resolved remotely — we connect securely to your computer and fix things while you watch. For hands-on work, we come to you. Same-day visits are available across Long Island for urgent needs.

For Homes & Small Businesses

We work with both residential clients and small businesses. Whether you need help setting up a home office, managing a small team's technology, or just keeping your family's laptops running smoothly — the service is personal, the pricing is transparent, and the jargon is nonexistent.

Why Choose MyCo IT?

We treat every client like our only client. No call centers, no ticket queues — just real people who know your setup and care about getting it right.
