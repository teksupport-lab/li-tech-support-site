Your technology, fully managed — set up, maintained, and supported by an advisor who explains everything in plain English. For homes and businesses across Long Island.

Your Technology, Taken Care Of

Whether you're working from home, running a business, or simply want your family's devices to work without headaches — we're here. MyCo IT provides hands-on, personal technology support that feels like having a trusted advisor on speed dial.

What We Help With

  • Computer and device setup, configuration, and optimization
  • Operating system updates, troubleshooting, and repairs
  • Email setup and productivity tool configuration
  • Data backup solutions and recovery planning
  • Printer, scanner, and peripheral setup
  • Smart home device integration
  • Software installation and licensing guidance
  • Cloud account management and migration

Security That Protects You

We protect your devices and data with the same tools leading organizations use — explained in plain English. From antivirus and malware protection to secure password management and two-factor authentication, we make sure you're covered without making it complicated.

Remote & On-Site Support

Many issues can be resolved remotely — we connect securely to your computer and resolve things while you watch. For hands-on work, we come to you. Same-day visits are available across Long Island for urgent needs. Based in Garden City, we serve all of Nassau and Suffolk County.

For Homes & Businesses

We work with residential clients and small businesses alike. Whether you need a home office set up properly, a small team's technology managed, or your family's laptops running smoothly — the service is personal, the pricing is transparent, and the jargon is nonexistent.
