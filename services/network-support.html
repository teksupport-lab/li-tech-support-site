Networks & Connectivity

Reliable WiFi, secure networks, and seamless connectivity across your home or office. We design, install, and manage it all.

Connectivity You Can Count On

Nothing is more frustrating than spotty WiFi or a network that drops at the worst time. MyCo IT designs and installs networks that actually work — whether you're streaming in the living room, video conferencing from your home office, or running a business with a dozen connected devices.

Home Networking

We set up whole-home WiFi systems that eliminate dead zones and keep every device connected reliably. From mesh networks to wired Ethernet runs, we design solutions tailored to your home's layout and your family's needs.

Business Networking

For small businesses, we build networks that support your team's productivity — secure guest WiFi, VPN access for remote workers, and infrastructure that grows with your business. All managed and monitored so problems get fixed before you notice them.

What We Handle

  • Whole-home and whole-office WiFi design and installation
  • Mesh network setup (Ubiquiti, Eero, and others)
  • Ethernet wiring and structured cabling
  • Router and firewall configuration
  • Network security and monitoring
  • VPN setup for secure remote access
  • Guest network configuration
  • ISP coordination and troubleshooting

Remote Support Available

Many network issues can be diagnosed and resolved remotely. We can check your network health, adjust settings, and troubleshoot connectivity problems without an on-site visit — though we're always happy to come by when hands-on work is needed.

Why MyCo IT for Networking?

We don't just plug in a router and hope for the best. We design networks that work throughout your entire space, explain what we're doing in plain English, and stick around to make sure everything runs smoothly.
