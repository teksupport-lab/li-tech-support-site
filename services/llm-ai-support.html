AI & LLM Guidance

Curious about ChatGPT, Claude, or AI tools for your business? We help you understand, adopt, and use AI effectively — at your own pace.

AI Made Approachable

The world of AI is moving fast, and it can feel overwhelming. That's where we come in. MyCo IT helps you cut through the hype and find the AI tools that actually make your life easier or your business more productive — without the jargon or pressure.

For Business Owners

We help small businesses on Long Island understand where AI fits into their workflow. Whether it's automating repetitive tasks, improving customer communication, or using AI to draft content — we'll show you what's possible and help you get started safely.

For Curious Individuals

Want to try ChatGPT or Claude but not sure where to start? We offer one-on-one guidance sessions where we walk you through these tools at your pace. No judgment, no rushing — just a patient, knowledgeable guide who makes it fun.

What We Cover

  • Introduction to AI assistants (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and others)

  • Prompt writing and getting better results from AI tools
  • AI safety, privacy, and best practices
  • Integration with your existing tools and workflows
  • Ongoing guidance as AI tools evolve

Remote Sessions Available

AI guidance sessions work great over video call. We can walk you through tools, share screens, and answer questions in real time — from wherever you are on Long Island.
